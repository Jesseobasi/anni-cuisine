Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by clicking any "Order Now" button on our site. This will take you to our official Google Form to fill out your request.

How far in advance do I need to order?

For standard menu items, please order at least 24 hours in advance. For large catering orders, we require at least one week's notice.

What are your delivery/pickup options?

We currently offer pickup at our [Your City] location. Local delivery is available for catering orders over [Your Price].

What is your cancellation policy?

As per our policies, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required for catering. Cancellations must be made 72 hours in advance. We do not offer refunds on regular orders, but please contact us if you have an issue.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We do our best! Please specify any allergies or dietary restrictions (e.g., gluten-free, vegan, nut-free) in the "Notes" section of the order form, and we will contact you to confirm.
