Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our Menu, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Once you submit your order inquiry, we will review it and contact you to confirm payment details and delivery/pickup times.

How far in advance do I need to order?

For standard orders, we require at least 24 hours notice. For large catering events or weddings, we recommend booking at least 1 week in advance to ensure availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept Zelle, Cash App, and Venmo. Payment details will be provided once your order inquiry is received. A 50% deposit is required for large catering orders.

What size are the trays?

All items on our menu are sold as Large Trays. These generally feed between 10 to 15 people depending on portion sizes. If you need specific dimensions, please contact us.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer delivery for a flat fee of $4.99 within our local radius. Pickup is always free. You can select your preference during checkout.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Changes or cancellations must be made at least 72 hours before your scheduled pickup/delivery time. Deposits for catering orders are non-refundable if cancelled within this window.

Do you accommodate allergies?

We take allergies seriously. Most items list key allergens (e.g., Peanuts, Shellfish, Gluten) in the description. However, our kitchen handles various ingredients. Please leave a note in the checkout form if you have a severe allergy.

How should I reheat my food?

For aluminum trays, we recommend reheating in an oven at 350°F (175°C) for 15-20 minutes or until hot. Keep the lid on to retain moisture. For fried items (like wings or plantains), you may want to uncover them for the last 5 minutes to crisp them up.
